01 — Overview

An address cast with intent.

Aurum takes its name from gold — the metal refined, never rushed. Aurum Abir carries that discipline into architecture: two high-rise towers set above a landscaped amenity deck, arranged around light, air and a considered sense of arrival. Every residence is planned for a household that has outgrown the ordinary.

In Dhanakawadi, an established south Pune neighbourhood prized for its everyday connectivity, Aurum Abir offers the rare pairing of a settled address and a freshly conceived tower — built for those who value both.
